About Us

IEEE SB CEC is the student branch of IEEE at the College of Engineering Chengannur. We aim to foster a community of passionate tech enthusiasts and leaders, driving innovation and learning through collaboration.

What is IEEE?

IEEE is a global association of professionals working towards technological advancement and the betterment of society through innovative solutions.

Vision

To foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Mission

To be essential to the global technical community and professionals everywhere, making a significant impact on improving global conditions.
